Research Interests

My research interest is Quantum Chromodynamics (QCD) in finite temperature, specifically, in an extremely hot and dense nuclear matter -- Quark Gluon Plasma, which is believed to be the state of the Universe a few microseconds after the Big Bang, and was first detected in the labotorary in ultra-relativistic heavy-ion collisions.

My Ph.D project focuses on the transport properties of heavy quarks in heavy-ion collisions.
